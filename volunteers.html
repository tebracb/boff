Hey! Thanks for showing interest in volunteering with us!

Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival (B.O.F.F) will be a large-scale event, we’re expecting 5000 visitors daily. We cannot coordinate such a big event without enthusiastic volunteers. We’re looking for help in all sort of areas like helpers behind the bar, marketing team, event-builders or hosts. The teams will be assigned later, but if you already have a preference please let us know below.

What we offer for your contribution:

  • You can look behind the scenes of a large event
  • Taking part in the brainstorming-sessions and planning
  • B.O.F.F volunteer’s t-shirt
  • Free warm meal on your working days
  • Free drinks after your shift
  • Great atmosphere and a fun team
  • Opportunity to see some great movies :)
